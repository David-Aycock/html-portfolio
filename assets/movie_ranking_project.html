The Best Movies According to David

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Porco Rosso

This is my favorite anime. I love the beautiful imagery.

Trial of the Chicago Seven

Fantastic Aaron Sorkin film. Legal drama with an incredibly interesting plot.

The Wonderful Story of Henry Sugar

A short film directed by Wes Anderson based on a book by Roald Dahl. I've lost count how many times I've seen it.
